Shortlisted candidates will be called for an online test at PSG TECH Campus and
those who clear the online test have to attend an interview. Candidates with SPARK/Research attitude are admitted to the programmes.

A call for Test / Interview does not however confer any right of admission. Only candidates with very high academic record
and have SPARK NOT MARK are selected for admission.
